About Us

01/ ABOUT THE PROJECT

Ceramico - A cozy pottery studio, where various master classes are held for all ages!

Each person has a great opportunity to feel like a creator and create any beautiful product of their own accord. The most important thing is that the studio employs experienced craftsmen who will always prompt and help!

02/ PROJECT TASK

With the help of a minimalistic and localized design of the site, demonstrate the friendly atmosphere of the studio that this is a place where you can spend time with comfort and interest with your family, children and friends.

SOLUTION

During the work on the project, light pastel shades were used, which accurately emphasize the direction of this activity, convey a warm and cozy atmosphere. The design was created in a minimalist style and used different forms for photographs to better convey the theme.
