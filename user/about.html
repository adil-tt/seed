Our Story

Handcrafted with Passion

Founded in 2020, Ceramico began as a small studio in Art City. Our mission is to bring the beauty of handcrafted ceramics into modern homes. We believe that the objects we use every day should be beautiful, functional, and durable.

Every piece is thrown on the wheel, trimmed, glazed, and fired by our team of skilled artisans. We use locally sourced clay and food-safe glazes to ensure that our products are not only stunning but also safe for you and your family.
